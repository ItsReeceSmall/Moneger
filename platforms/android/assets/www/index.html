Help

This quick and simple help screen will show you how to use the app.

Add expense

Press the 'Add Expense' button and enter the name of the expense by pressing the type field and entering the expense name, then do the same for the value of the expense.

Expenses List

By swiping left on the expenses list you can either delete the expense to remove it from the calculation or you can modify it.

Starting Balance

Press the text field and enter the value of money you wish to work out with.

Remaining Balance

This shows the remaining money after expences are accounted for.
